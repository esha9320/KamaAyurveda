Grids

The grid structure is composed of a one column grid, a two column grid, a three column grid, and a four column grid. This grid is very flexible and can be changed depending on the content of any website. Although the grids can be used in a variety of ways, together, they help to unify content and create a sense of consistency through a website.

Typography

My typographic selecction inclde Adobe Garamond Pro and Interface.Adobe Garamond Pro is widely used for its exceptional readability, but at the same takes up less space than other fonts. Its italic form is known to accentuate its pleasing curves without being garish. Garamond is altogether a more pleasant font for the eyes, and lends a human character to the shape of the written word. InterFace was inspired by sans serif fonts of the 19th Century, subtly blended with humanist details for a softer look. From screen to signage, InterFace carries any message with flawless precision.

Product Card

Under the product description there's an Add to cart button which displays the products' price. Once the user clicks on it they will be taken to Products' Page.
